Phillip Island Budget Trip Itinerary (Free Activities)

☀️ Day 1 – Saturday

San Remo Pelican Feeding

Watch the famous pelican feeding show at the San Remo jetty.
Best time: 12:00 PM (feeding time)

Rhyll Inlet Walk

A flat, stroller-friendly nature walk to spot koalas and native birds.
Best time: Morning or early afternoon (cooler and quieter)

Churchill Island Walks

Explore easy walking trails and beautiful coastal scenery.
Best time: Mid to late afternoon (less crowded)

The Nobbies Boardwalk

Walk along the scenic boardwalk overlooking the rugged coast and seal colonies.
Best time: Late afternoon to just before sunset

☁️ Day 2 – Sunday

Phillip Island Botanic Gardens

Enjoy a peaceful walk through beautiful gardens with stroller-friendly paths and picnic areas.
Best time: Morning to mid-morning for a quiet, cool stroll.

Seal Rocks

Visit Seal Rocks for free to see Australian fur seals resting on the rocks by the shore. Great wildlife watching!
Best time: Late morning to early afternoon for best light and seal activity.

Red Rocks Beach

Quiet beach, stroller-accessible with tide pools and red-colored rocks.
Best time: Early afternoon, ideally near low tide to explore tide pools.

Ventnor Lookout

Scenic views from the car in wet weather. Great for photos.
Best time: Mid to late afternoon for soft light and clear views.

Return to Nobbies Boardwalk (Optional)

Enjoy another visit if the weather improves before heading home.
Best time: Evening around sunset or dusk for possible penguin sightings.
